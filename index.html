About PAWADI

Mandate. Acquire, install, improve, maintain, and operate water supply and distribution systems for domestic, industrial, municipal, and agricultural uses within the Municipality of Pasacao, per Presidential Decree No. 198.

Vision. Quality water service for a resilient, healthy, and progressive Pasacao.

Mission. Deliver safe and reliable water, practice responsible resource management, and uphold transparency and citizen‑centric service.
